Judging Guidelines & Standards

🔍 Visual Assessment

Time: 5-10 minutes
Process: Examine under proper lighting. Note trichome coverage, color vibrancy, structure, and trim quality. Use magnifying glass for detailed inspection.

👃 Aroma Evaluation

Time: 3-5 minutes
Process: Break apart sample gently. Note initial scent, complexity of terpene profile, and overall pleasantness. Rest nose between samples.

🔥 Consumption Testing

Time: 15-20 minutes
Process: Use provided apparatus. Note initial flavor, smoothness, harshness, and aftertaste. Rate immediately after consumption.

⚡ Effects Documentation

Time: 60-120 minutes
Process: Monitor onset time, intensity, duration, and quality of effects. Complete evaluation within 2-hour window.

⚠️ Critical Judge Requirements

  • Maintain sample anonymity at all times
  • Do not discuss samples with other judges
  • Complete all evaluations within deadline
  • Report any issues immediately to organizers
  • Use standardized scoring criteria consistently
