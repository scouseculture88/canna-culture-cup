Evaluation Process

Our rigorous 6-step judging process ensures consistency and fairness across all evaluations

1

Blind Sample Prep

All entries are anonymized and assigned random codes to eliminate bias

2

Visual Assessment

Initial evaluation of structure, color, trichomes, and overall appearance

3

Aroma Analysis

Detailed assessment of terpene profiles and scent characteristics

4

Consumption Testing

Controlled evaluation of flavor, smoothness, and immediate effects

5

Effect Documentation

Recording onset, duration, intensity, and quality of effects over time

6

Final Scoring

Compilation of all criteria scores and comprehensive feedback

Category-Specific Standards

Flower Standards

Moisture Content

Optimal range: 8-12%. Properly cured flower should have slight give when squeezed but spring back. Not brittle or overly moist.

Trichome Quality

Abundant, milky-white trichomes with minimal amber. Clear trichomes indicate premature harvest, while excessive amber suggests over-maturity.

Trim Quality

Minimal sugar leaf presence, clean cuts, no machine damage. Hand-trimmed preferred for premium categories.

Concentrate Standards

Consistency

Appropriate texture for extraction method. Shatter should be glass-like, wax should be malleable, rosin should be creamy.

Color & Clarity

Light amber to golden color preferred. Dark colors may indicate oxidation or poor starting material.

Purity

Minimal residual solvents (if applicable), no visible contaminants, clean extraction process evident.

Edible Standards

Dosing Accuracy

Consistent dosing across servings, clearly labeled potency, effects matching stated THC/CBD content.

Taste Quality

Cannabis flavor should enhance, not overpower. No bitter or unpleasant cannabis taste unless intended for specific product.

Onset & Duration

Predictable onset time (typically 30-120 minutes), duration appropriate for dosage, consistent effects.

Vape Standards

Vapor Quality

Smooth, clean vapor production. No harshness, burning, or off-flavors. Consistent vapor density.

Hardware Performance

Reliable heating element, consistent temperature control, no clogging or leakage issues.

Oil Quality

Clean, properly decarboxylated oil. Appropriate viscosity, no separation or crystallization.

Topical Standards

Absorption Rate

Appropriate absorption for product type. Lotions should absorb smoothly, balms should provide lasting barrier.

Skin Feel

Non-greasy finish (unless intended), pleasant texture, no irritation or adverse reactions.

Effect Delivery

Localized effects as intended, appropriate potency for therapeutic claims, consistent relief duration.
